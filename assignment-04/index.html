Sand Dollars

Cheryl Lehman

Treasured bits from beneath the sea,
Could they be underwater currency?!
Star shapes have blessed their outer canvas.
Were they used as exchange in Atlantis?!

Long lost, buried without discourses,
Washed ashore by unearthly forces.
The moon beckons them to their seaside grave,
Out in the world for humans to crave.

Exposed to elements outside the ocean realm
Their beautiful parts are given to overwhelm.
Meaning to overcome the human heart,
Hoping to find them whole and not apart.
